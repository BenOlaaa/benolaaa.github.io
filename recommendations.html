Walking Pad & Treadmill Guide — What to Look For

My clients use walking pads for Zone-2 and desk steps. Here’s what matters most:

  • Deck length: 43–47″ for walking; 50″+ if you have a longer stride.
  • Noise: under ~60 dB if you share space or take calls.
  • Max speed: 3–4 mph is fine for walking; you don’t need 7–10 mph.
  • Controls: remote or app; safety auto-stop is a plus.
  • Storage: under-sofa height & wheels make it effortless to put away.

Scales & Tracking — Keep It Simple

Weekly trends beat daily swings. Pair a smart scale with a food scale so protein and calories match your plan.
