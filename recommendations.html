Walking Pad & Treadmill Guide — What to Look For

Clients use walking pads for Zone-2 and desk steps. Here’s how to choose the right one:

  • Deck length: 43–47″ works for walking; go 50″+ if you’re taller or have a longer stride.
  • Noise: under ~60 dB keeps calls & roommates happy. Brushed motors tend to be louder over time.
  • Max speed: 3–4 mph is enough for walking and Zone-2; running speeds add cost & size.
  • Controls & safety: simple remote/app, auto-stop, and a stable deck beat flashy features.
  • Storage: wheels + under-sofa height means you’ll actually put it away and keep using it.
